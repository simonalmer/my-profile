Web development

Designing and building websites for various ideas has always been something I've found very satisfying and entertaining. I've actually built a few wordpress websites before, such as a page for journalism or my own gardening firm. However, I relied on ready-made themes and plugins such as Elementor for drag-and-drop building which can be limiting and places constraints on your ideas. Also, with no knowledge it took many weeks to make... Therefore, I find it so interesting and helpful to finally learn HTML and CSS! I look forward to be able to build my own ideas from scratch, and to look over source code and know what to alter.

Gardening & plants

My grandparents have a lovely classical Swedish farmers house outside of the city, and it was here where my passion for gardening was born. By helping out at a young age, I found how calming it was to take care of nature. It's also rewarding and gives me a great relief from thinking too much in academic studies ;) I decided to turn this passion into a business so I'm very happy! The first mission when entering my 12 square meter Chambre de bonne in Montmatre was of course to buy some plants at IKEA!

Coffee

If there's something that will always cheer me up it's a great cup of coffee. I grind my own beans and my favorite is a classic scandinavian drip coffe, similar to "americano". I've worked as a barista at local coffee shops in Sweden and my dream is to have my very own espresso machine at home. I recommend trying turkish coffee with cardamom prepared in a traditional way of heating it with warm sand. Check it out!
